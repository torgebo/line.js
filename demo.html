line.js can draw two different kinds of graphs. Nothing more, nothing less. It can draw a simple, large line graph. Or it can draw a collection of sparklines, as coined by Edward Tufte. Below are examples of both.

And now for the sparklines! For these to look as good as possible on all screen resolutions, I recommend you draw them in collections of 12.
